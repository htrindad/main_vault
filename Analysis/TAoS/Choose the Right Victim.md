## Unity

You shouldn't seduce everyone. Only those who are either open to your charms, or that have already seduced you in one way or another (ideally, both these cases should be true to a degree if not fully). Do not take into consideration their lexical responses, but look into their unconscious drives. That person also fills you in some way, and vice-versa.
