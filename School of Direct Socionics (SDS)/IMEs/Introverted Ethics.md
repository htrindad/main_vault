## Description by Hugo Parada

Introverted ethics, is the awareness of implicit relations between 2 objects. How close person A is to person B, awareness of attraction, love or hate, care or indifference, loyalty, and a sense of tribalism, a sense of belonging (in terms of loyalty), empathy and sympathy. People who have this element in a [[lexical_instinctive|mental function]] are aware of the implicit ties between those around them, who is loyal to whom, who swears allegiance, who they are sided with. As a [[dynamic_static|static]] element, it takes the object's data at a specific point in time, and saves it as a snapshot, and as a [[context_content|introverted]] element, it takes 2 data points as input, before coming to an output. It does not evaluate the object, but the relation.

People who are [[kinetic_potential|strong]] ![[Fi.png|15]], are very charming by nature, and know how to make people like them, and how to manipulate the ties of other people according to their will. They possess the innate quality of [[The Charmer|charm]], unlike their ![[Fe.png|15]] cousins, who have the quality of [[The Charismatic|charisma]].
