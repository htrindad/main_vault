## Description by Hugo Parada

Introverted ethics, is the awareness of implicit relations between 2 objects. How close person A is to person B, awareness of attraction, love, hate, loyalty, and a sense of tribalism, a sense of belonging (in terms of loyalty), empathy and sympathy.
