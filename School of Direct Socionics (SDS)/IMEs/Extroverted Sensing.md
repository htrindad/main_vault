## Description by Hugo Parada

Extroverted sensing is the notion of the physical appearance and visual stimuli expressed by objects. It also deals with resources, possessions of people and things, as well as one's own will power, it is also the element of goals, objectives, use of force, and imposition, beautiful or ugly, style or unstylish, resourceful or broke[^1], color and shape, tenacious, or yielding. People who have this element in a [[lexical_instinctive|mental function]] are aware of the factors regarding to gravity, the object's appearance, it's power, and it's physical look (ugly or pretty), how tenacious is an individual, power dynamics. It's also not just about will and appearance, but resourcefulness, how much or how little do they have. As a [[dynamic_static|static]] element, it takes the object's data at a specific point in time, and saves it as a snapshot, and as an [[context_content|extroverted]] element, it takes the object itself as is, without the necessity of another object into the equation, and as such, it only evaluates that one object.

People who are [[kinetic_potential|strong]] ![[Se.png|15]] are very literal with meaning, and lack the patience to read between the lines. Something that is more close to ![[Ni.png|15]] or ![[Ne.png|15]] types.[^2]
